## Hlavná myšlienka
- Maurer review je prakticka a citatelna opora pre screeningovo-diagnosticku vetvu: zdoraznuje, ze screening ma zmysel len s navazujucim diagnostickym a liecebnym systemom, PHQ-2 je vstupny filter a pozitivny screen musi byt potvrdeny PHQ-9 alebo klinickym rozhovorom a DSM kriteriami.

## Kľúčové evidenčné bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract
- locator: PDF p. 1, `Abstract`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "The U.S. Preventive Services Task Force (USPSTF) and American Academy of Family Physicians recommend screening for depression in the general adult population. ... The PHQ-2 has sensitivity comparable with the PHQ-9 in most populations; however, the specificity of the PHQ-9 ranges from 91% to 94%, compared with 78% to 92% for the PHQ-2. If the PHQ-2 is positive for depression, the PHQ-9 or a clinical interview should be administered."
- parafráza: Toto je velmi pouzitelne pre odlisovanie dvoch krokov: PHQ-2 je rychly filter, ale diagnosticky proces sa tym nekonci a treba ho doplnit detailnejsim nastrojom alebo rozhovorom.
- use: opora pre screening-first, diagnosis-second logiku v uvode

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: introductory burden framing
- locator: PDF p. 1, first page main text
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Depression affects an estimated 8% of persons in the United States ... Globally, more than 300 million persons of all ages have depression, which is the leading cause of disability worldwide. ... If left untreated, depression may lead to suicide."
- parafráza: Review ukotvuje depresiu ako bezny a vysoko zavazny problem s verejnozdravotnym aj klinickym dopadom, co legitimuje potrebu spolahliveho vcasneho zachytu.
- use: uvodny epidemiologicky a klinicky anchor

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: Symptoms
- locator: PDF p. 2-3, `Symptoms`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Symptoms of depression are commonly known by the SIGECAPS mnemonic ... Depressed mood and anhedonia are the two cardinal symptoms of depression. The presence of four SIGECAPS symptoms plus depressed mood or anhedonia suggests depression, and further screening should be considered."
- parafráza: Maurer paper dava strucny a klinicky pouzitelny symptom frame: centralne su depressed mood a anhedonia, ale cele posudenie stoji na sirsom symptomovom obraze.
- use: premostenie od depresivnej symptomatiky k screeningovej logike

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: Screening for Depression in Adults
- locator: PDF p. 3-4, `Screening for Depression` + start of page 4
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "The USPSTF recommends screening for depression in the general adult population. Screening must be implemented with adequate systems in place to ensure accurate diagnosis, effective treatment, and appropriate follow-up. ... The PHQ-2 is accepted as an initial screening tool in all age groups. If depression is identified, the PHQ-9 or a clinical interview should be completed."
- parafráza: Klucom nie je iba screenovat, ale screenovat v systeme, kde existuje diagnosticke potvrdenie, liecba a follow-up. To je dolezite aj pre interpretaciu symptom-scale vystupov.
- use: argument proti zamenaniu screening tool za diagnozu

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: older-adult tools + diagnosis section
- locator: PDF p. 5, `Geriatric Depression Scale` + `Diagnosis of Depression`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "The five-item Geriatric Depression Scale ... was found to have a sensitivity of 94% and specificity of 81% ... The PHQ-2 can be used in the older population ... When screening is positive for possible depression, the diagnosis should be confirmed using DSM-5 criteria."
- parafráza: Review pripomina, ze ani pri starsich dospelych nejde len o jeden univerzalny nastroj; screening instrument sa voli podla kontextu, ale pozitivny vysledok sa stale musi prelozit do klinickych kriterii.
- use: metodicka opora pre rozlisovanie nastroja a klinickeho usudku

### Blok 6
- opiera sa o: DSM-5 criteria + bipolar exclusion
- locator: PDF p. 6, `DSM-5 Diagnostic Criteria for Major Depressive Disorder` + following paragraph
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "The occurrence of the major depressive episode is not better explained by schizoaffective disorder ... There has never been a manic episode or a hypomanic episode. ... Bipolar disorder must also be excluded, especially if the patient has ever had a manic or hypomanic episode."
- parafráza: Paper explicitne podciarkuje, ze diagnoza MDD zahrna aj vylucovanie konkurencnych vysvetleni a najma bipolarnych epizod; pozitivny depresivny screen sam osebe nestaci.
- use: opora pre diferencialnu diagnostiku v uvode

## Kľúčová definícia / výsledok / argument
- Screening na depresiu ma zmysel len ako vstup do diagnostickeho procesu; pozitivny PHQ screen treba potvrdit klinickym rozhovorom a DSM kriteriami a odlisit od inych stavov vratane bipolarity.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Review je practice-oriented a americky guideline-framed; nepouzival by som ho ako jediny zdroj pre psychometriu PHQ ani pre epidemiologiu.
- Cast odporucani je orientovana na primary care a specificke populacie, nie priamo na vyskumne settingy.
