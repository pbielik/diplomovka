## Kľúčové evidenčné bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract
- locator: PDF p. 1, `Abstract`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "The total satisfaction with learning subscale score ranged between 5 and 25 with a mean score of 19.36 ± 6.32. The total self-confidence subscale score ranged between 8 and 40 with a mean score of 30.87 ± 9.1. ... Approximately 91.7% of the students recommended using simulation."
- parafráza: Štúdia poskytuje súčasný pragmatic-use signal, že standardized patient training v psychiatric nursing je študentmi prijímaný veľmi pozitívne a je spájaný s vyššou sebadôverou.
- use: empirická opora pre prijateľnosť a perceived utility simulácie

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: Introduction
- locator: PDF p. 2, `Introduction`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Simulation ... is essential in preparing nursing students for real-world clinical scenarios. In psychiatric nursing, SPs allow students to improve their knowledge and competencies in addition to learning from errors by engaging with actual people through interactions and obtaining tangible feedback on how they perform. Moreover, SP simulation enhances students' preparedness for clinical placement with actual patients by decreasing their levels of anxiety and stress by increasing their knowledge, confidence, and interpersonal communication skills."
- parafráza: Intro paperu jasne prepája SP simuláciu s tým, čo je dôležité aj pre tvoju tému: učenie cez interakciu, bezpečnú chybovosť, okamžitý feedback a znižovanie stresu pred kontaktom s reálnym pacientom.
- use: teoretické odôvodnenie SP simulácie v psychiatric education

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: Introduction + local context rationale
- locator: PDF p. 2-3, end of `Introduction`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Only one large governmental specialized psychiatric mental health hospital ... provides clinical training ... this situation places a huge overload on the patients ... and the medical and administrative staff ... Another crucial aspect is the unique nature of psychiatric patients ... the stigma of psychiatric illness still has a major effect ... Therefore, standardized patient simulation must be utilized ..."
- parafráza: Tento paper je užitočný aj preto, že nehovorí o simulácii abstraktne. Ukazuje praktické dôvody, prečo je simulation attractive: obmedzená klinická kapacita, ochrana pacientov, confidentialita a stigma.
- use: opora pre argument, že simulácia rieši aj logistické a etické bariéry praxe

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: Results
- locator: PDF p. 4-9, `Results`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "42.9% of participants reported that they were extremely or very comfortable with interviewing and managing a patient with a mental health problem in a real clinical setting. More than half ... were either slightly or not at all comfortable ... Pearson's correlation coefficient ... revealed a statistically significant positive relationship between the participants' satisfaction with the learning experience and their self-confidence ... Data analysis confirmed a statistically significant positive correlation between the students' recommendations of simulation use ... with the total SSLS ... and their satisfaction in learning subscale."
- parafráza: Výsledky sú užitočné na ukázanie, že comfort level pre reálnu psychiatriu nie je samozrejmý, a práve preto dáva simulácia zmysel ako premostenie medzi triedou a klinikou.
- use: praktický anchor pre preparedness gap a potrebu tréningového mosta

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: Open-ended responses
- locator: PDF p. 9-10, `Responses to Open-Ended Questions`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "The advantages highlighted were authenticity, practicality, comfort, safe learning environments, timesaving, access to information, cost-effectiveness, standardized teaching, varied exposure, skill development, and immediate feedback. ... The disadvantages were ... unclear or inconsistent responses from the simulated patient ... group dynamics ... inability to adhere to the scenario."
- parafráza: Kvalitatívna časť je užitočná, lebo neukazuje len benefity. Okrem autenticity, bezpečia a feedbacku pomenúva aj typické slabiny simulácie: nekonzistentné odpovede, skupinové rušenie a drift od scenára.
- use: vyvážený discussion anchor pre plusy aj limity SP simulácie

## Kľúčová definícia / výsledok / argument
- SP simulácia v psychiatric nursing môže zvyšovať spokojnosť a self-confidence v bezpečnom prostredí, ale jej kvalita závisí aj od toho, ako konzistentne sa drží scenára a ako je riadená skupinová dynamika.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Ide o convenience sample z jedného univerzitného prostredia a self-report outcomes.
- Štúdia neukazuje priamy dopad na pacientské outcomes.
