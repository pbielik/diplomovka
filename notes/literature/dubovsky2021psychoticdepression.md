## Klucove evidencne bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract + Introduction
- locator: PDF p. 1, Abstract + opening Introduction
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Psychotic depression was initially considered to be at one end of a continuum of severity of major depression. Subsequent experience demonstrated that psychosis is an independent trait that may accompany mood disorders of varying severity."
  >
  > "psychotic depression remains a common condition that is often underrecognized and inadequately treated."
- parafraza: Autori hned v uvode odmietaju stare chapanie psychotickej depresie len ako maximalnej zavaznosti depresie. Psychoticke priznaky sa maju brat ako samostatny klinicky trait, ktory vie byt prehliadany aj pri beznej depresivnej prezentacii.
- use: klinicky anchor pre to, preco treba pri depresivnom rozhovore cielene pracovat aj s psychotickymi red flags

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: Evolution of the Concept of Psychotic Depression
- locator: PDF p. 2, section `Evolution of the Concept of Psychotic Depression`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "In DSM-5, psychotic depression is still a subtype of MDD rather than a separate disorder, but the psychosis specifier is independent of severity, so that depression does not have to be severe to justify a diagnosis of psychotic depression."
  >
  > "This change reflects an understanding that many depressive disorders can occur with or without psychosis, with different implications for treatment and prognosis."
- parafraza: Aj ked DSM-5 ponechava psychoticku depresiu ako subtype MDD, psychoticke prvky uz neviaze len na maximalnu zavaznost. To meni klinicku logiku: psychoticke prvky treba zistovat aj mimo najsilnejsich depresivnych epizod.
- use: opora pre klinicke odovodnenie, preco sa v rozhovore oplati sledovat psychoticke stopy aj pri nejednoznacnej symptomovej prezentacii

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: A Transdiagnostic Psychosis Trait
- locator: PDF p. 3, section `A Transdiagnostic Psychosis Trait`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "there is some evidence that the content of delusions and hallucinations may be similar in psychotic relatives of psychotically depressed patients, regardless of the actual psychotic disorder ... suggesting a transdiagnostic psychosis phenotype that overlaps affective and nonaffective psychotic syndromes"
  >
  > "Such research suggests that psychosis is a trait that can occur in a variety of disorders including schizophrenia, schizoaffective disorder, bipolar disorder, MDD"
- parafraza: Paper posuva psychozu z uzkej nozologickej kolonky do sirsieho transdiagnostickeho ramca. Pre differential diagnosis je to dolezite, lebo psychoticke prvky nemaju automaticky znamenat jednu konkretnu diagnozu.
- use: jemna brzda proti prilis zjednodusenemu citaniu psychotickych prvkov v diskusii alebo klinickom framingu

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: mood incongruence discussion + Psychotic Depression and Bipolar Disorder
- locator: PDF p. 4, end of mood incongruence discussion + start of `Psychotic Depression and Bipolar Disorder`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "the bulk of evidence suggests that there is no difference in outcome between psychotic depression with mood congruent versus mood incongruent symptoms"
  >
  > "One point about which there is growing consensus is that mood incongruent psychotic symptoms convey greater likelihood of bipolar than unipolar depression."
- parafraza: Mood incongruence sama osebe nemusi znamenat horsi priebeh, ale je uzitocny differential-diagnostic signal smerom k bipolarite. V rozhovore teda nefunguje len ako marker zavaznosti, ale aj ako diagnosticka stopa.
- use: pri framingu differential diagnosis medzi unipolarnou a bipolarnou depresiou

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: Covert Psychotic Depression
- locator: PDF p. 5, section `Covert Psychotic Depression`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Depressed patients may minimize or not report psychotic symptoms because they do not think that they are abnormal, they do not want to be considered ill, or they want to be left alone."
  >
  > "some patients may not think abstractly enough to answer broad general questions ... but they may be forthcoming if they are asked about specific hallucinations and delusions."
- parafraza: Velmi prakticky bod paperu je, ze psychoticke symptomy sa casto nevynoria pri vseobecnej otazke. Pacient ich moze bagatelizovat, nepoznat ako patologicke alebo ich prizna az pri konkretnom dopytovani.
- use: silny klinicky argument pre to, preco ma rozhovor pracovat aj s konkretnymi, nie len siroko formulovanymi psychotickymi promptmi

### Blok 6
- opiera sa o: treatment synthesis + Future Directions
- locator: PDF p. 1, Abstract + PDF p. 14, closing treatment section
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Acute treatment of psychotic unipolar depression generally involves the combination of an antidepressant and an antipsychotic drug or electroconvulsive therapy."
  >
  > "the goal of treatment of psychotic depression should be complete remission of both depression and psychosis"
- parafraza: Paper pripomina, ze psychoticka depresia nie je len diagnosticky detail. Ma dosledky pre liecbu, ktora sa orientuje na plnu remisiu depresivnych aj psychotickych symptomov.
- use: sekundarne do diskusie pri klinickej zavaznosti spravneho rozpoznania

## Klucova definicia / vysledok / argument
- Najsilnejsi prinos paperu pre tuto pracu je klinicka pointa, ze psychoticke prvky v depresii treba aktivne a cielene zistovat; nie su len synonymom extremnej zavaznosti a casto su differential-diagnostic stopou, ktoru pacient nepovie spontanne.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Ide o review paper, nie o primarnu empiricku studiu diagnostickej presnosti.
- Paper je siroky a klinicky orientovany; do rukopisu treba brat len tie body, ktore skutocne podporuju diferencialno-diagnosticky alebo klinicky framing.
