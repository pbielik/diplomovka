## Kľúčové evidenčné bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract + Introduction
- locator: PDF p. 1 / journal p. 1159, Abstract + Introduction
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Content validity is the most important measurement property of a patient-reported outcome measure (PROM) and the most challenging to assess. Our aims were to: (1) develop standards for evaluating the quality of PROM development; (2) update the original COSMIN standards for assessing the quality of content validity studies of PROMs; (3) develop criteria for what constitutes good content validity of PROMs, and (4) develop a rating system for summarizing the evidence on a PROM's content validity and grading the quality of the evidence in systematic reviews of PROMs."
- parafráza: Autori explicitne tvrdia, že content validity je najdôležitejšia measurement property PROM-u a zároveň najťažšia na posúdenie. Cieľom článku je preto vytvoriť štandardy, kritériá a rating system pre jej hodnotenie.
- use: silná metodická veta na otvorenie validačnej logiky rating nástroja

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: Introduction
- locator: PDF p. 2 / journal p. 1160, Introduction
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "All items in a PROM should be relevant for the construct of interest (within a specific population and context of use) and the PROM should be comprehensive with respect to patient concerns. Furthermore, the PROM should be understood by patients as intended. Lack of content validity can affect all other measurement properties. A high Cronbach's alpha is no guarantee that the construct of interest is being measured or that no important concepts are missing."
- parafráza: Content validity je definovaná ako miera, do akej obsah nástroja adekvátne odráža konštrukt, pričom sa hodnotí cez relevance, comprehensiveness a comprehensibility vzhľadom na konštrukt, cieľovú populáciu a kontext použitia. Autori súčasne zdôrazňujú, že bez content validity môžu byť skreslené aj ostatné measurement properties a vysoká Cronbachova alfa sama osebe nestačí.
- use: obhájenie, prečo nestačí reportovať len reliabilitu a treba riešiť aj obsahovú primeranosť položiek

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: Table 4
- locator: PDF p. 8 / journal p. 1166, Table 4
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Relevance: 1 Are the included items relevant for the construct of interest? 2 Are the included items relevant for the target population of interest? 3 Are the included items relevant for the context of use of interest? 4 Are the response options appropriate? 5 Is the recall period appropriate? Comprehensiveness: 6 Are all key concepts included? Comprehensibility: 7 Are the PROM instructions understood by the population of interest as intended? 8 Are the PROM items and response options understood by the population of interest as intended? 9 Are the PROM items appropriately worded? 10 Do the response options match the question?"
- parafráza: Table 4 prekladá content validity do 10 konkrétnych kritérií. Relevance zahŕňa relevanciu položiek pre konštrukt, populáciu a context of use, aj vhodnosť response options a recall period; comprehensiveness sa pýta, či sú zahrnuté všetky kľúčové koncepty; comprehensibility pokrýva zrozumiteľnosť inštrukcií, položiek a response options.
- use: priame premapovanie na ratingový nástroj a expert review checklist

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: Table 4 + Table 5 + Discussion
- locator: PDF p. 8-9 / journal p. 1166-1167, Table 4 + Table 5 + Discussion
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "The starting point is always that there is high quality evidence (on a given aspect of content validity). This level of evidence can be downgraded of one or more levels (to moderate, low or very low), if there is (serious or very serious) risk of bias, unexplained inconsistency in results, and/or indirect findings. The thresholds for defining serious or very serious pitfalls can be determined by the review team. However, we recommend to evaluate the internal structure of the PROM as a next step, after evaluating content validity."
- parafráza: COSMIN rozlišuje dve vrstvy hodnotenia: najprv rating obsahu samotného nástroja, potom grading kvality evidencie, ktorý štartuje na high a znižuje sa pre risk of bias, inconsistency a indirectness. V diskusii autori výslovne odporúčajú riešiť content validity skôr než internal structure.
- use: poradie krokov v metodike, ak budeš vysvetľovať, prečo ide expert content review pred sofistikovanejšími psychometrickými analýzami

## Kľúčová definícia / výsledok / argument
- Dobre obhájená content validity potrebuje nielen "relevantné položky", ale aj preukázanú comprehensiveness a comprehensibility vzhľadom na zamýšľaný konštrukt a použitie.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Text je písaný pre PROMs, nie priamo pre expert rating tool hodnotiaci kvalitu interview.
- Prenos do diplomovky preto musí byť explicitne analogický: nepreberať COSMIN mechanicky, ale použiť jeho logiku pre obsahovú obhajobu položiek a domén.
