## Hlavná myšlienka
- Zeng review je silny prehlad LLM-based virtual patients v medical education: pole rastie velmi rychlo, ale hodnotenie je stale metodicky slabe, zamerane hlavne na user experience, s velmi malym podielom validovanych nastrojov a objektivne meranych learning outcomes.

## Kľúčové evidenčné bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract
- locator: PDF p. 1, `Abstract`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "A total of 28 studies were included in this scoping review. Analysis revealed that 92.9% (26/28) of the studies were published in the past 2 years ... evaluation methods lack standardization, and only 13% (3/23) of studies used validated tools ... while only 21.7% (5/23) of studies objectively measured learning outcomes ... they overlook privacy and security considerations in practical applications."
- parafráza: Tento abstract je pre tvoju temu velmi uzitocny, lebo jednou vetou zhrna tri veci: pole je nove a rychlo rastie, evaluacia je metodicky slaba a privacy/security vrstva zaostava.
- use: high-level framing pre virtual-patient branch v uvode

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: Instructional Design Features
- locator: PDF p. 8, `Instructional Design Features`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "the most frequently reported scenario was taking a history inventory with an LLM-based virtual patient (20/23, 87%). ... No studies involved building a relationship with the patient or explaining and planning. All studies focused solely on verbal communication skills, without addressing nonverbal behaviors such as gestures or nodding."
- parafráza: Review ukazuje, ze vacsina LLM virtual-patient studii je dnes orientovana hlavne na verbalne history-taking scenare. Relationship-building, explanation/planning a nonverbalne vrstvy ostavaju slabo pokryte.
- use: opora pre limitaciu, ze aktualne AI pacienti pokryvaju iba cast klinickej komunikacie

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: learning outcomes and workshops
- locator: PDF p. 8, same section
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "In total, 21.7% (5/23) of the studies focused on aspects aimed at improving learning outcomes ... For instance, Brügge et al demonstrated that the intervention group receiving AI-generated personalized feedback performed better in clinical decision-making ... Such workshops facilitated better learning outcomes after practicing with LLM-based virtual patients."
- parafráza: Pozitivne signaly pre learning outcomes existuju, ale review ukazuje, ze objektivne meranie ucenia je stale mensinova vetva v porovnani s jednoduchsimi user-experience evaluaciami.
- use: vyvazeny argument pre potencial aj limity educational use-case

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: Principal Findings
- locator: PDF p. 9, `Discussion - Principal Findings`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Overall, the included studies demonstrated a positive attitude toward the application of LLM-based virtual patients. ... However, the heterogeneity in study designs, evaluation standards, learning outcomes, and their measurements limits the ability to make direct comparisons and draw definitive conclusions."
- parafráza: Zeng review je dobry antidot proti prehnanemu optimizmu: pozitivne dojmy uzivatelov su bezne, ale stale to nie je to iste ako pevny dokaz edukacnej efektivity.
- use: discussion anchor pre odlisovanie prijatia technologie od skutocneho ucinku

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: nonverbal communication limitation
- locator: PDF p. 12, section o nonverbal communication
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "the reviewed studies mostly focused on verbal communication, with limited attention to nonverbal behaviors. These nonverbal elements often carry as much, if not more, information than verbal communication itself ... achieving the goal of training nonverbal communication skills remains challenging."
- parafráza: Toto je pre tvoju diplomovku velmi relevantny limit. Aj ked textovy alebo verbalny simulated patient vie byt uzitocny, nedokaze plne pokryt nonverbalne signaly, ktore su v klinickej komunikacii casto klucove.
- use: limitation sekcia a opora pre realisticke vymedzenie scope AI simulacie

### Blok 6
- opiera sa o: Evaluation of LLM-Based Virtual Patients
- locator: PDF p. 11, `Evaluation of LLM-Based Virtual Patients`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "The design and evaluation of the 23 studies on the practical application of LLM-based virtual patients are heterogeneous and often inconsistent ... only a few studies used validated tools to assess LLM-based virtual patients, indicating a lack of standardization in the evaluation process. Furthermore, evaluations have largely focused on subjective indicators-users' experiences."
- parafráza: Review pomaha pomenovat metodicky problem poľa: velka cast evaluacie stoji na subjektivnych dojmoch a nema standardizovane validated measures, co oslabuje porovnatelnost medzi studiami.
- use: silna opora pre potrebu explicitnejsieho rating a validation designu

### Blok 7
- opiera sa o: Privacy and security + ethics
- locator: PDF p. 13-14, `Privacy and security` + `The Ethics of Using LLM-Based Virtual Patients`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Most of the studies used cloud-based LLMs ... concerns about data security and user privacy ... Local deployment of LLMs is also a reliable method for addressing data leakage and privacy concerns. ... Using LLM-based virtual patients with patient data raises core questions about ownership, consent, and anonymization."
- parafráza: Zeng paper neprinasa len edukacny prehlad, ale aj velmi prakticky ethics/governance layer: cloudove modely, ownership, consent, anonymizacia a local deployment su realne deployment otazky, nie iba abstraktna etika.
- use: ethics / governance framing pre discussion

## Kľúčová definícia / výsledok / argument
- LLM-based virtual patients maju zjavny potencial pre communication-skills training, ale aktualna evidencia je prilis heterogenna a prevažne user-experience based na to, aby sa z nej robili silne tvrdenia o edukacnej ucinnosti alebo plnej klinickej realite simulacie.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Review je z medical education domény, nie z cisto psychiatrickeho alebo psychologickeho trainingu; pri prenose na tvoju temu treba zachovat doménovu opatrnost.
- Cast zahrnutych studii je velmi nova a technologicky heterogenna, takze pole sa moze rychlo menit.
