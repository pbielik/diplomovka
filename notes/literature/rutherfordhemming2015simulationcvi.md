## Klucove evidencne bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: opening rationale + When to obtain content validity
- locator: PDF p. 1-2, opening text + `WHEN TO OBTAIN CONTENT VALIDITY IN SIMULATION SCENARIO ASSESSMENT`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "validity ... needs to be established prior to the scenario's use in clinical assessment or in research. A nonvalidated simulation scenario may lead to findings that are not accurate."
  >
  > "When simulation scenarios are incorporated into the design of a research study, they should be assessed for CV before being used."
- parafraza: Rutherford-Hemming hovori velmi priamo: simulacny scenar nie je len organizacny material, ale metodicky artefakt. Ak sa pouziva na hodnotenie alebo vo vyskume, obsahova validita sa ma riesit vopred.
- use: silny precedent pre to, preco je logicke validovat aj simulačne stimulusy alebo scenarovy material

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: definition of content validity in simulation
- locator: PDF p. 2, section `Content validity concerns...`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Content validity concerns the extent to which items on a tool, survey, or, in this case, a simulation scenario, represent an adequately prepared definition of a concept."
  >
  > "If the simulation scenario is not valid, it can skew the findings of the study, rendering the results meaningless."
- parafraza: Paper explicitne stavia simulacny scenar vedla nastroja alebo survey. Ak scenar nereprezentuje cielovy koncept dostatocne dobre, ohrozuje validitu celeho study workflowu.
- use: metodicka opora pre premostenie medzi expert review nastroja a expert review scenarov

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: the Lynn method
- locator: PDF p. 3, section `the lynn method`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Lynn recommends that at least six experts review the content in an instrument or simulation scenario."
  >
  > "Each item is evaluated for validity using a four-point ordinal scale: 1 = not relevant, 2 = somewhat relevant, 3 = quite relevant, and 4 = highly relevant."
  >
  > "the CVI is computed as the number of experts giving a rating of 3 or 4 divided by the total number of experts who evaluated the item."
- parafraza: Toto je najpraktickejsia cast paperu: Lynn-based CVI sa da pouzit aj pre simulation scenarios a paper ho prepise do velmi konkretneho postupu s poctom expertov, 4-bodovou relevancnou skalou a vypoctom item-level CVI.
- use: priamy anchor pre metodicky opis expert content review pri simulacnych materialoch

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: item and scale thresholds + Lawshe method
- locator: PDF p. 4, pasaz po priklade item CVI + start of `the lawshe method`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Lynn (1986) recommends that each item CVI be at least .78"
  >
  > "Polit and Beck (2006) and Davis (1992) indicate that a scale CVI of .80 or higher is acceptable."
  >
  > "Lawshe uses a three-point rating scale: 3 = essential, 2 = useful, but not essential, and 1 = not necessary."
- parafraza: Paper nevysvetluje len mechaniku, ale dava aj orientacne prahy. Pre simulacny scenar tak mas v jednom zdroji item-level hranicu cez Lynn a alternativny essentiality framing cez Lawshe.
- use: pri interpretacii, co by sa v podobnom expert review povazovalo za prijatelny vysledok

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: developing the CV form + Adding rigor to validation procedures
- locator: PDF p. 4-5, `Developing a CV Form for Content Experts` + `Adding Rigor to Validation Procedures`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "The entire simulation scenario needs to be written so experts can fully understand the entirety of content and accurately assess whether items in the simulation are relevant."
  >
  > "Include an open-ended question at the end of the CV form"
  >
  > "Be sure content experts are truly experts"
  >
  > "Send the CV form to experts anonymously when possible"
- parafraza: Rutherford-Hemming nejde len po skore. Dolezita je aj forma expert review: scenar musi byt napisany kompletne, treba zbierat otvoreny feedback, panel ma byt naozaj expert a anonymita moze zvysit kritickost komentarov.
- use: velmi dobra opora pre proceduralnu rigoroznost expert review workflowu

## Klucova definicia / vysledok / argument
- Paper robi presne to, co tato praca potrebuje: prenasa content validity z vseobecnej psychometrie do prostredia simulacnych scenarov a ukazuje, ako sa da CVI rozumne reportovat pri simulation-based materialoch.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Ide o kratky practice-oriented paper, nie o rozsiahlu psychometricku review.
- Prahy a postupy su uzitocne metodicke orientacne body, nie absolutne univerzalne pravidla pre kazdy dizajn.
