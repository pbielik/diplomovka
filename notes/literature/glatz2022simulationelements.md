## Klucove evidencne bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: abstract
- locator: SAGE page lines 111-114
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Results showed that students on the new curriculum reported lower levels of perceived competence before the clinical practicum, but increased significantly more over time"
- parafraza: Simulation-rich curriculum neviedol k jednoduchemu okamzitemu pocitu istoty, ale studenti nasledne pocas practica rasti v percepcii kompetencie rychlejsie.
- use: argument, ze simulacne prvky mozu fungovat ako priprava na neskorsie kompetencne dozrievanie

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: introduction
- locator: SAGE page lines 119-123
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Simulation has the purpose of enhancing students' educational experiences and offer opportunities for learning and examination that are authentic to students' future profession."
- parafraza: Autori explicitne ramcuju simulaciu ako nastroj na autentickejsie ucenie a evaluaciu profesijne relevantnych kompetencii.
- use: teoreticka opora pre edukacny framing simulacii

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: introduction
- locator: SAGE page lines 129-132
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "simulation-based elements increase students' confidence in applying their learning to real-world settings"
- parafraza: Glatz paper sa priamo opiera o predchadzajucu australsku evidenciu, ze simulacne prvky zvysuju confidence pri prenose ucenia do realnejsich situacii.
- use: most k paperu `[@sheen2021simulationeducation]`

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: discussion / conclusion
- locator: SAGE page lines 248-250
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "students who had done the OSCE felt less competent at the start of the clinical practicum but increased more ... students with better performance on the OSCE were perceived by their supervisors as more prepared"
- parafraza: Dolezite nie je len seba-hodnotenie studentov, ale aj to, ze lepsi OSCE performance sa spajal s tym, ze ich supervizori vnimali ako pripravenejsich na practicum.
- use: silna opora pre tvrdenie, ze simulacne prvky mozu podporovat practicum readiness

## Klucova definicia / vysledok / argument
- Simulation-based prvky mozu byt v psychologickom kurikule uzitocne nielen ako testovanie, ale aj ako sucast dlhsieho rozvoja profesionalnej kompetencie.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Autori opakovane upozornuju, ze vysledky su exploratorne a metodologicky limitovane.
- Nejde o LLM alebo virtual clients, ale o sirsie simulation-based prvky vratane OSCE.
