## Hlavna myslienka
- Paper obhajuje CVI ako prakticky a zmysluplny indikator obsahovej validity, ale len ak sa neberie ako holy percent agreement: item-level CVI treba citat spolu s korekciou na nahodnu zhodu a scale-level validity je rozumnejsie sumarizovat cez `S-CVI/Ave` nez cez `S-CVI/UA`.

## Klucove evidencne bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract
- locator: PDF p. 1, Abstract
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "the widely-used CVI has advantages with regard to ease of computation, understandability, focus on agreement of relevance rather than agreement per se, focus on consensus rather than consistency, and provision of both item and scale information. One weakness is its failure to adjust for chance agreement."
- parafraza: Autori odporucaju CVI nie preto, ze by bol psychometricky dokonaly, ale preto, ze drzi fokus na zhodu o relevancii poloziek, dava item-level aj scale-level informaciu a je metodicky komunikovatelny. Kriticke miesto je iba to, ze sam o sebe nekoriguje nahodnu zhodu.
- use: silna uvodna veta pre obhajobu, preco ma expert content review cez CVI zmysel

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: Abstract + Current status of the CVI
- locator: PDF p. 1-2, Abstract + `CURRENT STATUS OF THE CVI`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "To calculate an item-level CVI (I-CVI), experts are asked to rate the relevance of each item, usually on a 4-point scale... the I-CVI is computed as the number of experts giving a rating of either 3 or 4, divided by the number of experts."
  >
  > "One approach is to require universal agreement among experts... We refer to this approach as S-CVI/UA... Another approach... is to compute the I-CVI for each item on the scale, and then calculate the average I-CVI across items... S-CVI/Ave."
- parafraza: Paper velmi presne definuje prakticky postup: experti hodnotia relevanciu poloziek na 4-bodovej skale, z coho sa rata `I-CVI`, a scale-level validity sa potom da zhrnut bud cez `S-CVI/UA` alebo cez priemer `I-CVI` napriec polozkami (`S-CVI/Ave`).
- use: pri metodickom opise toho, co by znamenalo reportovat item-level a scale-level obsahovu validitu

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: Some alternatives to the CVI + Content validity and chance agreement
- locator: PDF p. 3-5, `SOME ALTERNATIVES TO THE CVI` + `CONTENT VALIDITY AND CHANCE AGREEMENT`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "A high value of alpha can be obtained even in situations in which agreement about content validity is low."
  >
  > "general indexes of inter-rater agreement... are problematic as measures of content validity because they capture agreement of any type, including agreement about the low relevance of an item."
- parafraza: Polit et al. vyslovene oddeluju content validity od beznej interrater consistency. Vysoka alfa alebo vseobecna zhoda este neznamena, ze sa experti zhodli na obsahovej relevantnosti polozky; mozu sa zhodovat aj na tom, ze polozka relevantna nie je.
- use: metodicka brzda proti tomu, aby sa obsahova validita zamenila s reliabilitou alebo vseobecnym agreementom

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: Table 4 + formulas for chance-corrected interpretation
- locator: PDF p. 7-8, Table 4 + section around `k*`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "After adjustments for chance, any I-CVI greater than or equal to .78 (e.g., seven out of nine experts) would also be considered excellent, which we think is an appropriate goal."
  >
  > "The safest generalization, then, is that any I-CVI greater than .78 would fall into the range considered excellent, regardless of the number of experts."
- parafraza: Najpraktickejsi vystup paperu je interpretacne pravidlo pre item-level validity: po korekcii na nahodnu zhodu sa `I-CVI >= .78` da brat ako velmi dobra az excelentna obsahova validita polozky.
- use: ak budes chciet v metode alebo diskusii pomenovat, co by bol rozumny ciel pre expert item review

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: Recommendations
- locator: PDF p. 8-9, `RECOMMENDATIONS`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "The first iteration of expert content validation would ideally involve review by a large panel of experts-perhaps 8-12 of them."
  >
  > "In the second round, a smaller group of experts (perhaps 3-5) can be used to evaluate the relevance of the revised set of items and to compute the S-CVI."
  >
  > "We think that the goal for the S-CVI/Ave should be .90."
- parafraza: Autori odporucaju iterativny expert review: sirsi prvy panel na reviziu poloziek, potom mensi druhy panel na potvrdenie relevance revidovanej verzie a na vypocet scale-level CVI. Ako ciel pre `S-CVI/Ave` navrhuju hodnotu `>= .90`.
- use: pri navrhu alebo retrospektivnej obhajobe, ako by sa dal rating tool validovat systematickejsie nez len opisnym expert feedbackom

## Klucova definicia / vysledok / argument
- Ak sa pri autorskom ratingovom nastroji ide cestou expertnej obsahovej validity, najsilnejsia prakticka logika z tohto paperu je: sledovat item relevance cez `I-CVI`, interpretovat ju s korekciou na nahodnu zhodu a scale-level sumarizovat radsej cez `S-CVI/Ave`, nie cez rigidny universal agreement.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Paper je metodicky, nie psychologicky klinicky; hovori o content validity vseobecne, nie o AI interview quality tools.
- Odporucane expert pocty a `CVI` prahy su prakticke heuristiky, nie absolutne pravidla, a treba ich citat spolu s tym, co bolo v projekte realne urobitelne.
