## Klucove evidencne bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract + Introduction
- locator: PDF p. 1, Abstract + Introduction
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "The Delphi method is a technique of social research that seeks to obtain a reliable collective opinion from a group of experts."
  >
  > "its classic version is characterized by (a) an iterative process ... (b) participant anonymity ... (c) controlled feedback ... and (d) a statistical group response"
- parafraza: Landeta vracia Delphi k jeho klasickym jadrovym prvkom: iteracia, anonymita, kontrolovany feedback a skupinova odpoved. To je dobra brzda proti tomu, aby sa akakolvek skupinova konzultacia automaticky volala Delphi.
- use: presne pomenovanie, co by musel splnat plny Delphi dizajn a preco tvoj expert review moze byt skor Delphi-like nez plny Delphi

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: rationale for indicators
- locator: PDF p. 3, concluding part of Introduction
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "no set of indicators or points of contrast showing and measuring the degree of quality of a Delphi study has been consolidated in the academic literature"
  >
  > "this paper seeks to offer a reasoned proposal for a synthetic set of indicators that could be included in any published study"
- parafraza: Paper vznikol z velmi konkretneho problemu: Delphi studies sa publikuju, ale casto bez kratkej, citatelnej vrstvy, podla ktorej by sa dala odhadnut kvalita ich prevedenia.
- use: opora pre to, preco je rozumne transparentne reportovat parametre expert panelu a review procesu

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: proposal of indicators for the expert panel
- locator: PDF p. 6, section starting `The first group of indicators...`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "The first group of indicators should therefore refer to the value of the group of experts who have participated in the study"
  >
  > "a reference could be 5-10 experts, per type of expert ... or 15-30, for homogeneous panels"
  >
  > "Aspects to consider include ... Quality of the experts ... Representativeness ... Motivation: percentage of dropouts between rounds"
- parafraza: Najpouzitelnejsia cast paperu je zoznam panel-level indikatorov: pocet expertov, ich kvalita, reprezentativnost a motivacia alebo dropout. Presne toto su veci, ktore sa daju v metode kratko a transparentne reportovat.
- use: checklist pre opis expert panelu alebo pre obhajobu, co v nom bolo a co nie

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: indicators of interaction quality
- locator: PDF p. 7, points 3-5 of the third indicator group
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Three to 4 weeks per round appears to be an acceptable period"
  >
  > "percentage of experts who have changed their opinion per question; variation in each round in the measures of central tendency and dispersion used; variation in the degree of consensus"
  >
  > "External evaluation of the key elements of the study ... is very rare to find in published Delphi applications."
- parafraza: Landeta ide dalej nez k opisu panelu. Chce vidiet aj to, ci sa nazory menili, ci boli kola dost stabilne a ci samotny proces hodnotili aj experti alebo ini uzivatelia zvonku.
- use: pri rozhodovani, co este ma zmysel reportovat, ak by sa expert review do buducna formalizoval viac Delphi smerom

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: concluding synthesis + Table 2
- locator: PDF p. 8, concluding section + start of Table 2
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "it is essential to include a limited set of quality indicators that are easy to appraise and simple to understand, measure genuinely relevant aspects of the quality of the research and are as objective and free from the bias of the researcher"
  >
  > "It focuses on three areas that are particularly relevant to the quality of a Delphi study: the quality of the panel of participating experts, the way in which valuable information is obtained from the experts, and the quality of the interaction generated between the experts."
- parafraza: Zaver paperu je velmi prakticky: netreba reportovat vsetko, ale treba reportovat malu sadu jasnych a citatelnych indikatorov v troch oblastiach panel, elicitation a interaction.
- use: vysoka hodnota pre metodicky reporting styl, aj ked tvoj dizajn nebude plny Delphi

## Klucova definicia / vysledok / argument
- Ak sa expert review v praci opisuje ako Delphi-like alebo consensus-based, Landeta pomaha odlisit dojem od rigorozneho opisu: treba povedat nie len kto boli experti, ale aj ako sa od nich zbierali vstupy a ake znamky interakcie alebo stability proces vykazoval.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Paper je o kvalite Delphi studies vseobecne, nie o psychometrickej content validity rating nastroja.
- Odporucane pocty expertov a indikatory su reporting heuristiky, nie tvrde povinne minima.
