## Hlavna myslienka
- Cook je silny historicky anchor pre computer-based virtual patients: v health professions education maju velke efekty oproti ziadnej intervencii, ale oproti inym aktivnym nekomputerovym metodam su ich priemerne vyhody male. Dolezite preto nie je len "ci" virtual patients funguju, ale "ako" su navrhnute a implementovane.

## Klucove evidencne bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract + Introduction
- locator: PDF p. 1, Abstract + opening Introduction
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Educators increasingly use virtual patients (computerized clinical case simulations) in health professions training."
  >
  > "The computer-screen-based virtual patient, 'a specific type of computer program that simulates real-life clinical scenarios; learners emulate the roles of health care providers to obtain a history, conduct a physical exam, and make diagnostic and therapeutic decisions,' has been proposed as one way to develop these essential cognitive clinical skills."
- parafraza: Paper definuje virtual patients ako interaktivne pocitacove klinicke simulacie, v ktorych sa student sprava ako poskytovatel zdravotnej starostlivosti a robi anamnesticke, diagnosticke a terapeuticke rozhodnutia. Je to dobry pre-LLM definicny anchor pre simulačnú líniu.
- use: definicia a historicke ukotvenie virtual patient branch pred LLM

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: Abstract results
- locator: PDF p. 1, Abstract `Results`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Compared with no intervention, the pooled effect size... was 0.94 ... for knowledge outcomes, 0.80 ... for clinical reasoning, and 0.90 ... for other skills."
  >
  > "Compared with noncomputer instruction... effect size ... was 0.06 for knowledge, -0.004 for reasoning, and 0.10 for other skills."
- parafraza: Meta-analyticky signal je dvojity: virtual patients maju velky prinos oproti ziadnej intervencii, ale oproti inym aktivnym nekomputerovym metodam nemaju v priemere ziadnu velku prevahu. To podporuje opatrny framing "useful educational modality", nie "jednoznacne najlepsia metoda".
- use: hlavny empiricky anchor pre opatrne tvrdenia o edukacnom prinosu VP

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: Methods definition / inclusion logic
- locator: PDF p. 2, `Question` + `Data sources and searches`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "We defined a virtual patient as 'a specific type of computer program that simulates real-life clinical scenarios; learners emulate the roles of health care providers to obtain a history, conduct a physical exam, and make diagnostic and therapeutic decisions.'"
  >
  > "This excluded other forms of computer-based learning... and other forms of simulation such as standardized patients, manikins, part-task trainers, and systems requiring specialized equipment not found on a typical personal computer."
- parafraza: Autori presne oddeľujú virtual patients od inych simulacnych modalit, ako su standardized patients, manikiny alebo specializovana VR technika. To sa hodi pri terminologickom cisteni v uvode, ked potrebujes ukazat, ze tvoja linia patri do sirsej rodiny simulacii, ale nie je to to iste ako klasicky SP model.
- use: pojmove odlíšenie virtual patients od inych simulacnych pristupov

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: Qualitative studies synthesis
- locator: PDF p. 4-5, `Studies using formal qualitative methods` + Table 1
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "virtual patients have perceived advantages over other instructional approaches, including student independence, accommodation of student schedules, efficient contributions to the student's mental case library, and an unstressful learning environment. Yet, simultaneously, students believe virtual patients should not replace real patient experiences."
  >
  > "students identified multiple factors contributing to a case's realism, including the case material ... the type of case ... and the computer presentation"
- parafraza: Okrem pooled effect sizes paper zachytava aj to, preco su virtual patients pedagogicky atraktivne: podporuju samostatnost, budovanie mental case library a bezpecnejsie prostredie. Zaroven sa tu objavuje dolezita brzda, ze nemaju nahradit realnych pacientov a ze realizmus zavisi od autenticity scriptu, typu pripadu aj sposobu prezentacie.
- use: pre diskusiu realizmu, bezpečného nácviku a complement-not-replace framing

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: Discussion + direct comparisons of designs
- locator: PDF p. 9-11, `Comparisons between virtual patient designs` + `Discussion`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "mastery learning, advance organizers, enhanced feedback, and explicitly contrasting cases improved learning outcomes in randomized trials"
  >
  > "The associations found in several studies between changes intended to make the virtual patient more realistic and neutral or negative outcomes raise questions regarding for whom, in what contexts, and for what outcomes greater realism is beneficial."
- parafraza: Pre tvoju pracu je velmi uzitocne, ze Cook neposuva debatu len na uroven medium effect sizes, ale aj na uroven dizajnu. Vyssiu efektivitu podporuju mastery learning, advance organizers a feedback, kym vacsia "realistickost" sama osebe nemusi automaticky zlepsit vysledky.
- use: opora pre simulačný rámec, kde dizajnové voľby a guardraily môžu byť dôležitejšie než samotná existencia simulácie

### Blok 6
- opiera sa o: Discussion + Conclusions
- locator: PDF p. 11-12, `Discussion` + closing `Conclusions`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "virtual patients, in comparison with no intervention, are consistently associated with higher learning outcomes."
  >
  > "Evidence does not indicate superiority of virtual patients over other training methods... they may be noninferior in some instances."
  >
  > "Further research clarifying how to effectively implement virtual patients is needed."
- parafraza: Zaver paperu je metodicky triezvy: virtual patients su obhajitelne ako uzitocny treningovy nastroj, ale literatura neukazuje ich univerzalnu superioritu. Klucova otvorena otazka nie je existencia modality, ale jej efektivna implementacia.
- use: discussion anchor proti prehnanym tvrdeniam o VP ako samospasitelnom rieseni

## Klucova definicia / vysledok / argument
- Cook 2010 je dobry historicky most medzi klasickou simulation literatúrou a tvojim LLM-based simulačným rámcom: ukazuje, že computer-based virtual patients už dlhšie dávajú zmysel ako tréningová modalita, ale ich prínos stojí na kvalite dizajnu a integrácii do výučby, nie len na samotnej technológii.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Review pokryva health professions education velmi siroko, nie specificky psychologicke alebo psychiatricke interview.
- Cast evidence je stara a heterogenna; paper je silny ako konceptualny a metodicky anchor, nie ako presny benchmark pre tvoju konkretnu domenu.
