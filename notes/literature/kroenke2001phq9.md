## Kľúčové evidenčné bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: subsection `The PHQ-9`
- locator: PDF p. 2 / journal p. 607, subsection `The PHQ-9`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "The PHQ-9 (Appendix) is the 9-item depression module from the full PHQ. Major depression is diagnosed if 5 or more of the 9 depressive symptom criteria have been present at least 'more than half the days' in the past 2 weeks, and 1 of the symptoms is depressed mood or anhedonia. As a severity measure, the PHQ-9 score can range from 0 to 27, since each of the 9 items can be scored from 0 (not at all) to 3 (nearly every day)."
- parafráza: Autori explicitne definujú PHQ-9 ako 9-item depression module z PHQ, kde každá položka zodpovedá jednému DSM-IV symptómu a skóruje sa od 0 do 3, takže celkové skóre je 0-27. Toto je základná opora pre symptom anchoring a severity logiku.
- use: obhájenie, prečo sa seed symptomatika a závažnosť môžu opierať o PHQ-9 logiku

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: section `Analysis` + section `Results`
- locator: PDF p. 3 / journal p. 608, section `Analysis` + `Results`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "For most analyses, the PHQ-9 score was divided into the following categories of increasing severity: 0-4, 5-9, 10-14, 15-19, and 20 or greater. These categories were chosen for several reasons. The first was pragmatic, in that the cut points of 5, 10, 15, and 20 are simple for clinicians to remember and apply. The internal reliability of the PHQ-9 was excellent, with a Cronbach's alpha of 0.89 in the PHQ Primary Care Study and 0.86 in the PHQ Ob-Gyn Study."
- parafráza: Článok zavádza päť severity intervalov `0-4`, `5-9`, `10-14`, `15-19` a `20+`, pričom tvrdí, že hranice 5, 10, 15 a 20 sú praktické pre klinikov a empiricky nemenia podstatu vzťahu medzi skórom a konštruktovou validitou. Na tej istej strane autori reportujú aj vysokú internú reliabilitu (`alpha = 0.89` v primary care a `0.86` v ob-gyn vzorke).
- use: anchorovanie severity pásiem a obhájenie, že PHQ-9 nie je len screening item list

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: Table 3 + surrounding results paragraph
- locator: PDF p. 4 / journal p. 609, Table 3
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Table 3 displays the sensitivity, specificity, and likelihood ratios for different PHQ-9 thresholds in diagnosing major depression in the 580 patients who had a MHP interview." Row for cutpoint 10: "`10 88 88 7.1`". "ROC analysis showed that the area under the curve for the PHQ-9 in diagnosing major depression was 0.95, suggesting a test that discriminates well between persons with and without major depression."
- parafráza: Pri hranici `PHQ-9 >= 10` vychádza senzitivita `88%` a špecificita `88%` pre major depression proti structured mental health professional interview. Na tej istej strane autori uvádzajú aj AUC `0.95`, čo podporuje použitie PHQ-9 ako silného orientačného severity a case-finding nástroja.
- use: keď bude treba v texte obhájiť prečo je hranica 10 klinicky zmysluplná referencia

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: Table 4 + Table 5 + results interpretation
- locator: PDF p. 4-5 / journal p. 609-610, Table 4 + Table 5
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "As shown in Table 4, there was a strong association between increasing PHQ-9 depression severity scores and worsening function on all 6 SF-20 scales. Several findings should be noted. First, results were essentially the same for both the primary care and obstetrics-gynecology samples. Table 5 shows the association between PHQ-9 severity levels and 3 other measures of construct validity: self-reported disability days, clinic visits, and the general amount of difficulty patients attribute to their symptoms. Greater levels of depression severity were associated with a monotonic increase in disability days, health-care utilization, and symptom-related difficulty in activities and relationships."
- parafráza: Vyššie PHQ-9 severity skóre je monotónne spojené s horším fungovaním na SF-20 škálach, viac disability days, vyšším health care utilization a väčšou symptom-related difficulty. To je kľúčový argument, že PHQ-9 zachytáva nielen symptómy, ale aj funkčný dopad závažnosti.
- use: keď budeš potrebovať premostiť symptom fidelity na klinickú plausibilitu a funkčný dopad

## Kľúčová definícia / výsledok / argument
- PHQ-9 spája symptom checklist a severity measure v jednom krátkom nástroji a jeho cut-offy majú aj empirickú, nielen praktickú oporu.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Vzorka je z primary care a ob-gyn settings a článok pracuje s DSM-IV dobovým rámcom.
- PHQ-9 nie je náhradou klinického interview; skôr stručný anchor pre symptom domény a závažnosť.
