## Klucove evidencne bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract + opening definition of content validity
- locator: PDF p. 1, Abstract + opening paragraph
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Evidence of content validity can be captured using qualitative and/or quantitative methods and is typically based on the judgment of individuals deemed to be experts in the field of study being targeted."
  >
  > "Too often, the specifics of how experts actually helped to shape the constructs addressed are left out; the reader is left with the assurance that content validity was addressed and established, without explicit details on how this was accomplished."
- parafraza: Dinnesen explicitne hovori, ze content validity nestaci iba deklarovat. Ak sa expert panel pouzije, treba ukazat aj konkretny proces, ako expertne vstupy menili obsah a strukturu materialu.
- use: silna metodicka veta pre obhajobu transparentneho expert review procesu

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: section discussing quantification of content validity
- locator: PDF p. 3, pasaz od `Although there is general consensus...`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "there is a lack of consensus regarding the best way to quantify content validity."
  >
  > "Regardless of the method, it remains important for intervention developers to utilize a systematic and quantifiable method for determining content validity."
- parafraza: Paper je uzitocny aj ako striezva metodicka poznamka: literatura nema jednu povinnu metriku obsahovej validity, ale aj tak je dolezite pouzit systematicky a kvantifikovatelny postup, nie volny expert comment bez struktury.
- use: opora pre to, preco je vhodne mat rubriku, rating a explicitny review workflow aj bez plneho Delphi dizajnu

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: Procedure
- locator: PDF p. 4, `Procedure`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "the structure for the collaboration included three parts: (a) initial expert review of developed materials, (b) a 1-day face-to-face meeting with the expert panel, and (c) subsequent expert review of revised materials to ensure that feedback was captured and incorporated accurately."
- parafraza: Najpraktickejsi prinos paperu je trojkrokovy expert-panel workflow: prvy individualny review, potom spolocna diskusia a nakoniec kontrolny second-pass na overenie, ze revizie naozaj zohladnili feedback.
- use: velmi dobry precedent pre opis iterativnej expertnej validacie stimulusov alebo rating toolu

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: Content validity rubrics + notepad questions
- locator: PDF p. 4-5, `Content validity rubrics` + `Notepad`; Appendix rubric on PDF p. 13
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Content validity scales or rubrics are often used to capture ratings on materials or assessments being developed."
  >
  > "The four open-ended questions included the following: 1. What works in this section? 2. What is challenging in this section? 3. Tell us about some suggestions you have. 4. What haven't we discussed?"
  >
  > "1 = This component is not addressed comprehensively ... 4 = This component is addressed comprehensively."
- parafraza: Dinnesen neostal len pri neformalnej debate. Pouzil rubriky na strukturovane hodnotenie a doplnil ich otvorenymi otazkami, aby zachytil aj feedback, ktory sa do skaly nezmesti.
- use: dobra opora pre kombinovanie skale + otvorenych komentarov v expert review

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: Data Analysis + Discussion
- locator: PDF p. 6 + p. 12, `Data Analysis` + Discussion
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "To calculate a quantifiable estimate of content validity, scores on each rubric were tallied and a mean score was calculated."
  >
  > "these rubrics provided structured data artifacts that guided experts in providing feedback and provided our team with consistent categories that allowed for comparison between experts and between data collection points"
- parafraza: Paper ukazuje, preco ma zmysel zbierat aj jednoduche kvantitativne ratingy: umoznia porovnavat expertov navzajom aj medzi kolami revizie a drzat stabilnu strukturu feedbacku.
- use: argument pre jednoduche sumarizovanie expert ratings aj pri malom expert paneli

## Klucova definicia / vysledok / argument
- Dinnesen je silny proceduralny anchor pre to, ze obsahova validacia moze byt iterativny expert-process artefakt: rubriky, meeting, otvoreny feedback, nasledna revizia a overenie, ze sa feedback naozaj pretavil do materialov.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Ide o validaciu edukacnej intervencie, nie klinickeho rating toolu pre AI interview.
- Paper neustanovuje univerzalny CVI standard; skor ukazuje dobre zdokumentovany expert-panel workflow.
