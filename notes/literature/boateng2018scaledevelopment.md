## Kľúčové evidenčné bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract + Introduction
- locator: PDF p. 1 / article p. 1, Abstract + Introduction
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Scale development and validation are critical to much of the work in the health, social, and behavioral sciences. However, the constellation of techniques required for scale development and evaluation can be onerous, jargon-filled, unfamiliar, and resource-intensive. Further, it is often not a part of graduate training."
  >
  > "We identified three phases that span nine steps. In the first phase, items are generated and the validity of their content is assessed. In the second phase, the scale is constructed. In the third phase, scale evaluation, the number of dimensions is tested, reliability is tested, and validity is assessed."
- parafráza: Boateng rámuje scale development ako viacstupňový proces s tromi fázami a deviatimi krokmi. To je dôležité pre metodickú argumentáciu, že rating tool nevzniká jedným psychometrickým ukazovateľom.
- use: silná úvodná metodická veta pre logiku konštrukcie nástroja

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: section `Scale development overview` + Fig. 1
- locator: PDF p. 2 / article p. 2, `Scale development overview`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "There are three phases to creating a rigorous scale—item development, scale development, and scale evaluation; these can be further broken down into nine steps. Item development, i.e., coming up with the initial set of questions for an eventual scale, is composed of: (1) identification of the domain(s) and item generation, and (2) consideration of content validity. The second phase, scale development, consists of (3) pre-testing questions, (4) sampling and survey administration, (5) item reduction, and (6) extraction of latent factors. The last phase, scale evaluation, requires: (7) tests of dimensionality, (8) tests of reliability, and (9) tests of validity."
- parafráza: Tento paper dáva veľmi použiteľnú mapu postupu: od domain definition a content validity až po dimensionality, reliability a validity. Je to praktický backbone pre vysvetlenie, kde v procese sa nachádza tvoj rating tool.
- use: prehľadová metodická kostra do Method alebo do validačného odôvodnenia

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: Table 1, Step 2 + Step 3 + Step 4
- locator: PDF p. 3 / article p. 3, Table 1
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Step 2: Content Validity: Assessing if the Items Adequately Measure the Domain of Interest. 2.1 Quantify assessments of 5-7 expert judges using formalized scaling and statistical procedures including content validity ratio, content validity index, or Cohen’s coefficient alpha. 2.2 Conduct Delphi method with expert judges. 2.3 Conduct cognitive interviews with end users of scale items to evaluate face validity."
  >

- parafráza: Pre tvoju diplomovku je kľúčové, že Boateng explicitne spája expert review, CVI-like kvantifikáciu, prípadný Delphi prvok a neskoršie pretesting s respondentmi. Zároveň dáva aj orientačné sample-size heuristiky pre neskoršie scale evaluation kroky.
- use: opora pre expert panel/content validity časť a pre oddelenie pilotnej obsahovej validácie od neskoršej psychometrie

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: section `Step 6: Extraction of Factors` + `Step 7: Tests of Dimensionality`
- locator: PDF p. 11-12 / article p. 11-12, `Step 6` + `Step 7`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "A number of scales developed stop at this phase and jump to tests of reliability, but the factors extracted at this point only provide a hypothetical structure of the scale. The dimensionality of these factors need to be tested before moving on to reliability and validity assessment."
  >
  > "Confirmatory factor analysis is a form of psychometric assessment that allows for the systematic comparison of an alternative a priori factor structure based on systematic fit assessment procedures and estimates the relationship between latent constructs, which have been corrected for measurement errors."
- parafráza: Boateng výslovne varuje pred častou chybou: po EFA alebo factor extraction sa nesmie hneď preskočiť na reliabilitu, lebo faktorová štruktúra je zatiaľ len hypotetická. Najprv treba otestovať dimensionality.
- use: silná opora pre poradie validačných krokov a proti skratke “spravili sme alfu a hotovo”

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: Table 2 + `Step 8: Tests of Reliability`
- locator: PDF p. 12-13 / article p. 12-13, Table 2 + `Step 8`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "RMSEA ≤ 0.06 ... TLI ≥ 0.95 ... CFI ≥ 0.95 ... SRMR ≤ 0.08."
  >
  > "Cronbach’s alpha assesses the internal consistency of the scale items, i.e., the degree to which the set of items in the scale co-vary, relative to their sum score. An alpha coefficient of 0.70 has often been regarded as an acceptable threshold for reliability; however, 0.80 and 0.95 is preferred for the psychometric quality of scales."
- parafráza: Článok je užitočný aj ako praktický zdroj prahov a indexov pre neskoršie testovanie model fit a reliability. Zároveň ale reliabilitu jasne zasádza až po dimensionality a pred validity.
- use: metodická opora, keď bude treba stručne pomenovať psychometrické prahy alebo logiku poradia testov

## Kľúčová definícia / výsledok / argument
- Dobrý rating nástroj má prejsť postupom od domain definition a content validity cez testovanie latentnej štruktúry až po reliabilitu a ďalšie validity checks; jednoduchý jednorazový ukazovateľ nestačí.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Ide o všeobecný primer pre scale development, nie o papier šitý na expert rating interview kvality.
- Sample-size heuristiky a psychometrické kroky treba prenášať opatrne, podľa toho, čo je v tvojej štúdii reálne realizovateľné a čo patrí skôr do ďalšieho vývoja nástroja.
