## Klucove evidencne bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract
- locator: PDF p. 1, Abstract
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "We conducted a three-stage consensus-building approach: (1) identifying the list of features ... (2) a two-round modified Delphi exercise with a panel of experts ... and (3) a small in-person consensus group meeting to finalise the list of features."
  >
  > "A list of 104 candidate questions was generated ... The exercise yielded 74 questions (71% of 104) on which there was consensus"
- parafraza: Woodcock ukazuje citatelny skeleton modified Delphi postupu: najprv vznikne kandidatny longlist, potom sa prezenie dvoma kolami expertneho ratingu a zvysok sa dorezi na finalnom konsenzualnom meetingu.
- use: velmi dobra opora pre opis viacstupnoveho consensus-building procesu

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: Delphi round 1 and round 2 methods
- locator: PDF p. 3, `Delphi round 1` + `Delphi round 2`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Panellists were then asked to vote to keep, remove, or modify the question, or to state that they had no opinion."
  >
  > "Consensus was set a priori at 75% agreement ... We excluded responses of 'no opinion' from percentage agreement calculations."
- parafraza: Pre tvoju pracu je dolezity proceduralny detail: experti nemuseli len suhlasit alebo nesuhlasit, ale mohli navrhnut aj modifikaciu. Zaroven bol consensus threshold nastaveny vopred a `no opinion` sa nezarataval do percent agreement.
- use: precedent pre transparentne nastavovanie thresholdov a response categories pri expert review

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: sample size rationale + results table
- locator: PDF p. 3-5, sample size paragraph + Table 1 + Table 2
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "We aimed to achieve a panel of 11-30 members, sample sizes in this range are typical for the Delphi method and have been shown to be effective and reliable"
  >
  > "Of the 23 panellists who consented to take part, 19 completed both rounds"
  >
  > "At the end of round 1, the panel had reached consensus on keeping 46 (44%) questions ... At the end of round 2 ... 22 and remove two"
- parafraza: Woodcock dava velmi konkretny priklad panelovej logiky: ciel 11-30 expertov, realne 19 completers, a potom postupne zuzenie 104 otazok cez dve kola consensus scoringu.
- use: uzitocny benchmark pre realisticke ocakavania od panel size a attrition pri modified Delphi

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: discussion of transparency vs quality
- locator: PDF p. 6, subsection `Methodological validity and transparency of the measurement plan`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "the questions should seek solely to address the transparency of a plan ... or should also seek to assess the quality of the approach to measurement."
  >
  > "it would be difficult ... to seek additionally to specify the means by which quality of the methods would be assessed"
- parafraza: Paper robi dolezite rozlisenie medzi dvoma urovnami: ci je plan transparentny a kompletne opisany, a ci je metodicky naozaj kvalitny. To nie je to iste a druha vrstva je casto ovela tazsia.
- use: opatrny anchor, ked budes v metode odlisovat dokumentovany expert review od plnej psychometrickej validacie

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: Discussion strengths/limitations
- locator: PDF p. 7, Discussion
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Use of the modified Delphi technique in Stage 2 offered a number of advantages, preserving the anonymity of panellists and allowing unrestricted expression of opinions"
  >
  > "the number of questions may be a risk to feasibility for general use"
- parafraza: Woodcock pripomina dve veci naraz: modified Delphi vie chranit anonymitu a podporit volnejsie vyjadrenie expertov, ale zaroven vie vyprodukovat aj velmi dlhy a menej prakticky checklist, ak sa neudrzi scope.
- use: vyvazena diskusna opora pre silne aj slabe stranky consensus-based designu

## Klucova definicia / vysledok / argument
- Woodcock je dobry priklad, ako sa da expert consensus operacionalizovat cez vopred definovany threshold, viacero response categories a staged narrowing z longlistu do praktickejsieho finalneho vystupu.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Paper je o measurement plans v QI, nie o klinickom rating nastroji ani o AI interview.
- Zaverecny vystup autorov je checklist features, nie plna validacna teoria; prenos do diplomovky je proceduralny, nie vecny.
