## Hlavná myšlienka
- Generative AI a foundation models môžu výrazne zasiahnuť kvalitu a bezpečnosť zdravotnej starostlivosti, ale ich prínos je neoddeliteľný od nových rizík: bias, hallucinations, dokumentačné chyby, regulačné otázky a nejasná reálna výkonnosť v klinickej praxi.

## Kľúčové evidenčné bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract + Introduction
- locator: PDF p. 1, Abstract + Introduction
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "It is also clear these capabilities have direct applicability to healthcare and to improving quality and patient safety, even as they introduce new complexities and risks. ... Foundation models (and their close relatives, generative AI and large language models) represent an important change: they are able to handle many different kinds of problems without additional datasets or training."
- parafráza: Howell rámuje foundation models ako kvalitatívny posun oproti task-specific AI. Práve táto generalita je zdrojom sľubu aj nových safety komplikácií.
- use: high-level anchor pre generative AI safety framing

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: explanation of foundation models
- locator: PDF p. 2, Table 1 + surrounding text
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Foundation model ... refers to large models that are trained on a wide variety of data types ... and that can be easily adapted to many different kinds of tasks without retraining. Foundation models represent a shift away from task-specific AI, towards AI that can handle many different types of tasks."
- parafráza: Tento paper je užitočný aj definitoricky: vysvetľuje, prečo sú foundation models iný typ technológie než staršie úzko trénované AI systémy.
- use: stručná definícia pre intro/discussion

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: section `Will AI help with quality and safety?`
- locator: PDF p. 4, `Will AI help with quality and safety? How?`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Overall, foundation models offer tremendous opportunity to improve health on an almost startling scale by democratising expertise to where it is needed. This means that quality and safety professionals should expect impact across all of the Six Aims: safety, effectiveness, patient-centredness, timeliness, efficiency and equity."
  >
  > "Generative AI’s earliest forays have focused on supporting, and in some cases removing, documentation burden for clinicians, which is a well-documented contributor to these issues."
- parafráza: Howell vidí silný potenciál hlavne v dokumentácii, meraní a sprístupňovaní expertízy. Zároveň to nepredáva ako úzko diagnostickú technológiu, ale ako širšiu infraštruktúru kvality a safety.
- use: vyvážený upside framing bez prehnanej klinickej extrapolácie

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: section `Risks and need for future research`
- locator: PDF p. 5, `Risks and need for future research`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "New technologies also bring new risks for quality and safety. ... Equity problems may arise from issues in biases in training data; language models recapitulating biased, sexist or racist misconceptions that lead to health disparities; issues with the algorithm’s design or implementation; and variations in performance of AI across different populations that were not reflected in model training."
  >
  > "Large language models have some surprising behaviour. ... they sometimes make things up—like journal references ... it is critical to remember that the basic versions of these models are not information-retrieval engines: the models themselves do not look things up in PubMed, they remember things out of their pretrained parameters that look like plausible next words in a sequence."
- parafráza: Toto je veľmi použiteľný safety blok: bias a hallucination nie sú okrajové technické chyby, ale jadrové riziká, ktoré priamo súvisia s tým, ako foundation models fungujú.
- use: opora pre discussion o hallucinations, bias a unsafe overtrust

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: ending discussion + practical caution
- locator: PDF p. 6, final page
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "We have seen truly remarkable progress already in just a single year, and it looks as if it will continue. ... quality and safety professionals will have to think creatively—about the upside for patients, families and clinicians; about the known and emerging risks; and about how these tools may affect their own practice."
- parafráza: Záver paperu odporúča nefixovať sa len na súčasný stav modelov. Práve tempo zmeny je samo osebe governance problém, lebo vyžaduje priebežnú reevaluáciu rizík.
- use: diskusný anchor pre ongoing monitoring a adaptive governance

## Kľúčová definícia / výsledok / argument
- Foundation models treba v zdravotníctve chápať zároveň ako nástroj kvality aj ako nový zdroj safety rizika; jedno bez druhého je zavádzajúce.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Ide o review/viewpoint paper, nie o primárnu evaluačnú štúdiu jednej konkrétnej aplikácie.
- Paper má aj deklarovaný priemyselný kontext autora, takže na konkrétne risk metrics je lepšie ho párovať s empirickými safety papers.
