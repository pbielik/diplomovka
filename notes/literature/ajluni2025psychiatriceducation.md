## Kľúčové evidenčné bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract + opening introduction
- locator: PDF p. 1, Abstract + opening paragraphs
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) in psychiatric education offers transformative potential to enhance clinical competence through realistic simulations. Traditional educational methods face limitations in replicating complex psychiatric cases, and AI-based tools provide a scalable solution. ... Findings suggest that AI can enhance psychiatric education by offering consistent, adaptable learning experiences that prepare trainees for real-world complexities. However, challenges such as ethical considerations and accessibility disparities must be addressed for AI to be effectively integrated into psychiatric training."
- parafráza: Ajluni paper je dobrý high-level framing zdroj pre tvrdenie, že AI simulácie sľubujú konzistentné a škálovateľné tréningové prostredie, ale už v abstrakte ich viaže na etické a access limity.
- use: úvodný framing pre AI simulácie v psychiatrickom vzdelávaní

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: discussion of psychiatric education problem + AI simulation affordances
- locator: PDF p. 2, `DISCUSSION`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Psychiatric education faces a unique dilemma: how to provide ample clinical exposure without risking patient well-being or compromising the quality of care. Traditional methods, such as role-playing or standardized patients, while valuable, have limitations in scalability, consistency, and the range of clinical scenarios they can effectively simulate. ... The potential benefits of AI simulations are manifold: they provide a safe learning environment for students to practice diagnostic and therapeutic skills, particularly for high-stakes scenarios like suicide risk assessment or acute psychosis management."
- parafráza: Text explicitne rámuje problém psychiatrického vzdelávania ako napätie medzi potrebou bohatej klinickej expozície a ochranou pacientov. AI simulácie sú tu predstavené ako riešenie pre bezpečný tréning high-stakes situácií.
- use: obhájenie, prečo AI simulácie dávajú zmysel práve v psychiatrii

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: discussion of standardization, feedback, and breadth of exposure
- locator: PDF p. 2, middle-to-late `DISCUSSION`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Unlike human standardized patients, AI simulations can consistently present the same case across multiple interactions, ensuring equitable training experiences. This consistency allows for standardized assessment of student performance and enables educators to track progress over time more accurately. Furthermore, AI simulations can generate a wide range of psychiatric presentations, including rare conditions or complex comorbidities that students might not encounter during limited clinical rotations."
- parafráza: Silná praktická výhoda, ktorú paper zdôrazňuje, je kombinácia štandardizácie a šírky prípadu. AI môže opakovať rovnaký case konzistentne a zároveň generovať aj zriedkavé alebo komplexné kombinácie stavov.
- use: opora pre simulated-patient argument o consistency a breadth of exposure

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: discussion of ethics, dehumanization risk, and implementation barriers
- locator: PDF p. 3, ethics and implementation paragraphs
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "The use of AI in simulating mental health conditions raises ethical questions about the authenticity of the experience and the potential for trivializing complex human experiences. There is a risk that over-reliance on AI simulations could lead to a dehumanization of psychiatric practice, potentially diminishing students’ ability to connect empathetically with real patients. To mitigate this risk, it’s crucial that AI simulations are used as a complement to, rather than a replacement for, interactions with real patients and human instructors."
  >
  > "Developing sophisticated AI simulations can be expensive, potentially creating disparities in access among institutions. This could lead to inequalities in the quality of psychiatric education, with students at well-funded institutions having access to more advanced training tools."
- parafráza: Ajluni nie je len techno-optimistický. Výslovne varuje pred trivializáciou psychických ťažkostí, dehumanizáciou praxe a prístupovou nerovnosťou medzi inštitúciami.
- use: diskusný anchor pre complement-not-replace a equity risk

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: recommendations + conclusion
- locator: PDF p. 4, recommendations list + concluding paragraphs
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "To harness the full potential of AI simulations in psychiatric education, I propose the following recommendations: 1. Develop a standardized framework for evaluating and implementing AI simulations in psychiatric curricula. ... 2. Establish ethical guidelines for the use of AI in simulating psychiatric conditions ... 3. Invest in research to assess the long-term impact of AI-enhanced training on clinical competence and patient outcomes."
  >
  > "It’s crucial to recognize that while AI can be a powerful educational tool, it cannot replace the importance of real human interaction in psychiatric training. The goal should be to use AI as a complement to, rather than a replacement for, traditional clinical experiences and human mentorship."
- parafráza: Záver paperu je použiteľný ako normatívny rámec: evaluácia, etické guidelines a dlhodobé outcome research majú predchádzať nekritickému nasadeniu AI simulácií.
- use: governance a implementation framing pre discussion

## Kľúčová definícia / výsledok / argument
- AI simulácie v psychiatrickom vzdelávaní majú zmysel hlavne ako bezpečný a štandardizovaný doplnok, nie ako náhrada reálnej klinickej skúsenosti.
