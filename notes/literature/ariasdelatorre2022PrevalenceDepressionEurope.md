## Kľúčové evidenčné bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: conference abstract - Objectives + Methods
- locator: PDF p. 1, abstract `Prevalence of depression in Europe using two different PHQ-8 scoring methods`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Data from 27 countries included in the European Health Survey (EHIS-2) for the year 2014/2015 were used (n=258,888). All participants who completed the PHQ-8 were included. The prevalence of depression ... were calculated ... using a PHQ-8>=10 cut-off point and the PHQ-8 algorithm scoring method."
- parafráza: Uz samotny dizajn abstractu je uzitocny: ide o velky europsky survey napriec 27 krajinami a priamo porovnava dva bezne sposoby scoringu PHQ-8.
- use: metodicky anchor pre to, ze prevalence zalozena na symptom scale nie je uplne nezavisla od scoring rule

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: conference abstract - Results
- locator: PDF p. 1, `Results`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "The overall prevalence of depression for all Europe was lower using the PHQ-8>=10 cut-off point (6.38%, 95%CI 6.24-6.52) than the PHQ-8 algorithm (7.01%, 95%CI, 6.86-7.16)."
- parafráza: Autori ukazuju, ze aj pri tom istom nastroji moze europsky odhad prevalencie depresie zretelne kolisat podla toho, ci sa pouzije jednoduchy cut-off alebo algoritmicke score.
- use: opora pre opatrnu interpretaciu prevalence a pre porovnanie scale-based odhadov

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: conference abstract - country-level results
- locator: PDF p. 1, `Results`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "Using the PHQ-8≥10 cut-off point, the highest prevalence was observed in Iceland ... Luxembourg ... and Germany ... Using the PHQ-8 algorithm the highest rates were observed in Hungary ... Portugal ... and Iceland ..."
- parafráza: Paper neprinasa len jedno globalne cislo; ukazuje aj to, ze poradie krajin sa meni podla scoring method, co este viac podciarkuje metodicku citlivost scale-based prevalence.
- use: doplnkovy epidemiologicky point pre europsky kontext

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: conference abstract - Conclusions
- locator: PDF p. 1, `Conclusions`
- kontrolný excerpt:
  > "There is variability in the prevalence of depression rates in Europe according to the PHQ-8 scoring method. These findings suggest the necessity of identify the method of choice for each country comparing with a gold standard measure (clinical diagnosis)."
- parafráza: Najdolezitejsi zaver pre tvoju pracu je metodicky: symptom-scale prevalence by sa mala validovat voci gold standardu, nie brat ako samozrejme porovnatelne cislo.
- use: opora pre distinction medzi screening instrument a klinickou diagnozou

## Kľúčová definícia / výsledok / argument
- Europska prevalence depresie odvodena z PHQ-8 nie je invariantna; zavisi aj od scoring strategy, preto treba symptomove skaly interpretovat opatrne a idealne voci klinickemu standardu.
