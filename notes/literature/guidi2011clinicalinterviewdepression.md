## Klucove evidencne bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract
- locator: PDF p. 1, Abstract
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "A comprehensive assessment of the wide spectrum of depressive symptomatology, particularly in its subclinical forms, is lacking in standard rating scales"
- parafraza: Autori otvaraju problem tym, ze bezne skaly nezachytavaju dostatocne siroke a jemne klinicke spektrum depresivnej symptomatiky, najma v subklinickych podobach.
- use: opora pre tvrdenie, ze pri depresii nestaci pracovat len s uzkym symptom checklistom

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: Abstract + Conclusion
- locator: PDF p. 1, Abstract + concluding sentence
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "broad evaluation of affective symptomatology and the sensitivity to change"
- parafraza: Ako hlavnu clinimetric vyhodu CID autori zdoraznuju sirku zachytenia afektivnej symptomatiky a citlivost na zmenu v case alebo po liecbe.
- use: opora pre argument, ze kvalitny interview framework ma sledovat aj jemne rozdiely a zmeny, nie len hrubu pritomnost symptomov

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: Introduction + Development of the CID
- locator: PDF p. 2, `Introduction` + `Development of the CID`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "a wide range of symptoms related to depressive syndromes and other psychiatric disorders including both psychic and somatic anxiety as well as phobias, obsessions, depersonalization, and psychotic features"
- parafraza: Vyvoj CID bol od zaciatku motivovany snahou zachytit depresiu v sirsom klinickom obraze, vratane komorbidnych a kontextovych prvkov, nie iba uzke jadro nalady.
- use: opora pre tvrdenie, ze klinicky vierohodny obraz depresie zahrna aj sirsi psychopatologicky kontext

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: description of scoring rationale + Table 1
- locator: PDF p. 3 / journal p. 12, text above Table 1 + Table 1
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "phenomenological accuracy necessary to discriminate a fleeting mood from serious and incapacitating affective disturbances"
- parafraza: Autori vyslovene zdoraznuju, ze interview ma odlisit prechodnu naladu od vaznejsej a funkcne zavaznej afektivnej poruchy. Tabulka zaroven ukazuje, ze pri depresii sleduju aj reaktivitu na socialne prostredie, work and interests, energiu, chut do jedla, spanok, suicidality a dalsie domény.
- use: pri obhajobe, ze realisticky rozhovor musi prepajat symptomy, disclosure, fungovanie a celkovy klinicky dojem

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: change version + discriminant validity sections
- locator: PDF p. 4-5 / journal p. 13-14
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "The individual items are suitable for use as separate outcome measures" ; "The CID discriminated sensitively between acute or partially remitted patients ... and normal subjects"
- parafraza: CID nie je len opisny interview format; paper ukazuje aj jeho uzitocnost pri citlivejsom odlisovani skupin a pri sledovani jednotlivych klinickych domen ako samostatnych outcome-ov.
- use: metodicka opora pre viacvrstvove posudzovanie kvality a symptom fidelity

## Klucova definicia / vysledok / argument
- Klinicke interview pri depresii ma hodnotit nielen to, ci sa v texte objavia "spravne" symptomy, ale aj ich kvalitu, sirku, zavaznost, kontext a funkcnu vahu v celkovom obraze pripadu.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Ide o review a clinimetric paper o CID, nie o specificky navod pre AI simulacie.
- Paper je silny na klinicke ramovanie depresivneho interview a na sirku domén, nie na modernu LLM alebo edukacnu literaturu.
