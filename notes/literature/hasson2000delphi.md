## Klucove evidencne bloky

### Blok 1
- opiera sa o: Abstract
- locator: PDF p. 1, Abstract
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "The Delphi survey is a group facilitation technique, which is an iterative multistage process, designed to transform opinion into group consensus."
  >
  > "It is a flexible approach ... yet little guidance exists to help researchers undertake this method of data collection."
- parafraza: Hasson dava jasnu pracovnu definiciu Delphi: iterativny viacstupnovy proces, ktory ma transformovat nazory expertov do skupinoveho konsenzu. Zaroven pripomina, ze flexibilita metody zvadza k nekonzistentnemu pouzivaniu.
- use: presne ukotvenie, co mozes a nemozes v metode nazyvat Delphi

### Blok 2
- opiera sa o: Issues involved in using the Delphi technique / Problem identification
- locator: PDF p. 2, `ISSUES INVOLVED IN USING THE DELPHI TECHNIQUE`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "to date no universal guidelines exist."
  >
  > "careful thought must be given before using the method; there are key issues surrounding problem identification, researcher skills and data presentation"
  >
  > "The Delphi is only appropriate to investigate certain research problems"
- parafraza: Paper vyslovene hovori, ze Delphi nie je default pre akukolvek expert konzultaciu. Najprv treba vediet, ci problem vobec vyzaduje group-consensus pristup a ci ma vyskum kapacitu metodicky ho udrzat.
- use: velmi dobra opora pre opatrne pomenovanie vlastneho expert review ako Delphi-like, nie automaticky ako plny Delphi

### Blok 3
- opiera sa o: Understanding the process
- locator: PDF p. 3, `Understanding the process`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "series of structured questionnaires (commonly referred to as rounds). The questionnaires are completed anonymously by these 'experts'..."
  >
  > "responses from each questionnaire are fed back in summarized form to the participants."
  >
  > "As with all good surveys, pilot testing with a small group of individuals should precede implementation."
- parafraza: Jadro procesu je v strukturovanych kolach, anonymite, sumarizovanom feedbacku medzi kolami a pilotazi. Bez tychto prvkov sa expert process vzdialuje od klasickeho Delphi.
- use: checklist pre odlisovanie plneho Delphi od jednoduchsieho expert review workflowu

### Blok 4
- opiera sa o: Finding a sample + recruitment and retention
- locator: PDF p. 3-4, `Finding a sample`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "Studies employing the Delphi make use of individuals who have knowledge of the topic being investigated"
  >
  > "a fine balance must be struck in selecting experts who will be relatively impartial ... yet also have an interest in the research topic."
  >
  > "the Delphi ... requires a continued commitment from participants being questioned about the same topic over and over again"
- parafraza: Paper dava prakticke kriterium pre panel: experti maju byt informovani, dostatocne kompetentni, ale nie tak zauzati, aby systematicky tahali proces jednym smerom. Zaroven treba pocitat s unavou a dropoutom napriec kolami.
- use: pri reporte vyberu expertov a limitov maleho alebo zatazeneho panelu

### Blok 5
- opiera sa o: Data analysis + validity discussion + Interpretation of findings
- locator: PDF p. 5-6, `Data analysis`, validity discussion and `Interpretation of findings`
- kontrolny excerpt:
  > "participants themselves should judge items in terms of quality, not the researchers."
  >
  > "the use of participants who have knowledge and an interest in the topic may help to increase the content validity of the Delphi ... and the use of successive rounds of the questionnaire helps to increase the concurrent validity."
  >
  > "the existence of a consensus does not mean that the correct answer, opinion or judgement has been found."
- parafraza: Hasson je uzitocny aj ako brzda proti preinterpretovaniu konsenzu. Delphi moze podporit obsahovu aj concurrent validity, ale samotny konsenzus este nie je dokaz pravdy a nema nahradit vecne argumenty.
- use: metodicke limity v diskusii a opatrny jazyk pri interpretacii expert zhody

## Klucova definicia / vysledok / argument
- Hasson je najlepsi ako reporting a framing anchor: ak expert proces nema jasne kola, anonymitu, kontrolovany feedback a premysleny panel, je bezpecnejsie volit oznacenie Delphi-like alebo consensus-based review.

## Limity alebo pozor
- Paper nedava jednu povinnu statisticku hranicu konsenzu ani psychometricky standard pre validaciu nastroja.
- Skor ako metricky paper je to prakticky metodicky navod a auditny ramec.
